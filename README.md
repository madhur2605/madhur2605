## 📂 Featured Projects

### 🌧️ Spatial Rainfall Interpolation — France
> *Python · QGIS · IDW · Kriging · Thin Plate Spline · NOAA GSOD / IMD NetCDF*

Conducted multi-method spatial interpolation of rainfall data for France (NOAA GSOD). Evaluated IDW, Ordinary Kriging, and TPS methods using RMSE and R² metrics. Extracted station-wise time-series data and generated interpolated raster maps for climate risk communication.

📁 **Repo:** [`spatial-rainfall-interpolation`](https://github.com/madhur2605/spatial-rainfall-interpolation)

---

### 🌊 Flood & Drought Risk Assessment — Ernakulam District
> *Python · NetCDF · IMD Data 1974–2023 · Pandas · Matplotlib*

Analyzed 50 years of IMD gridded rainfall data for Ernakulam district to identify historical flood and drought patterns, seasonal anomalies, and risk zones. Produced time-series trend analysis and spatial risk maps.

📁 **Repo:** [`ernakulam-flood-drought-risk`](https://github.com/madhur2605/ernakulam-flood-drought-risk)

---

### ♻️ Credit-Based Circular Economy Model — E-Waste Recovery, Mumbai *(Working Paper)*
> *Economic Modelling · Sustainability Strategy · ESG · SDG Alignment*

Proposed a credit-based incentive mechanism for high-value material recovery from e-waste in Mumbai. Integrated economic, social, and sustainability dimensions. Presented at IIT Bombay.

📁 **Repo:** [`ewaste-circular-economy-mumbai`](https://github.com/madhur2605/ewaste-circular-economy-mumbai)

---

### 📚 Bibliometric Analysis — Reusable Menstrual Products
> *R · Biblioshiny · VOSviewer · Web of Science / Scopus*

Conducted a systematic bibliometric study on the global research landscape of reusable menstrual products. Mapped keyword co-occurrence, author collaboration networks, and thematic clusters using VOSviewer.

📁 **Repo:** [`bibliometric-menstrual-products`](https://github.com/madhur2605/bibliometric-menstrual-products)

---

### 🏙️ Urban Immersion — Kochi GIS Dashboard
> *QGIS · GIS Spatial Analysis · KMC / GIZ*

Co-developed ward-level urban diagnostic indicators for Kochi Municipal Corporation with GIZ. Built spatial dashboards for urban planning inputs.
